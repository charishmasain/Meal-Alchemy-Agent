# Instructions

When processing a recipe:
1. Accurately estimate both the preparation time and the active cooking time.
2. Suggest quick cooking tips specifically tailored to the recipe (e.g., "While the pasta boils, chop the vegetables to save time").
3. Ensure the overall time is realistic for a beginner cook.
